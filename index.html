How to Play Tiki Solitaire

Moving Cards

  • Stack same rank on same rank (e.g., 9 on 9)
  • Stack cards that sum to 14 (A=1, J=11, Q=12, K=13)
  • Any card can go in an empty column

Making Pairs

  • Select two cards: one red, one black, summing to 14
  • Example: A♥ + K♣, 6♦ + 8♠
  • Hold up to 6 pairs

Building Dominos

  • Combine two pairs to form a four-suited bridge
  • Example: A♥K♣ + A♦K♠ = AK domino

Making Chains

  • Connect dominos by matching values
  • Example: AK77 connects to 772Q
  • Goal: longest chain (circular if all cards used)

Congratulations!
